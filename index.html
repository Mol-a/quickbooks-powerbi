QuickBooks Power BI Connector

Privacy Policy

This application only accesses your QuickBooks Online data to provide reporting and analytics through Power BI. Your data will not be stored, shared, or sold. You are responsible for managing your own QuickBooks Online account credentials.

End-User License Agreement (EULA)

By using this application, you agree that it is provided "as-is" for accessing your QuickBooks Online data through Power BI. The developer is not responsible for any loss or misuse of data. Use at your own risk.

Launch Instructions

  1. Open Power BI Desktop.
  2. Go to Get Data → More → Other → QuickBooks eStore Connector.
  3. Enter your Company ID (Realm ID) and follow the OAuth2 login flow.
  4. After authentication, select the desired endpoint (items, customers, invoices, etc.) to load data.

Disconnect Instructions

To disconnect this application from your QuickBooks Online account, go to your QuickBooks Online dashboard → Apps → find "QuickBooks Power BI Connector" → click Disconnect or Remove.
